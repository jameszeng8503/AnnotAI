AnnotAI Viewer

A browser-based viewer, annotator, presenter, and launcher for documents, media, URLs, AI tools, and social platforms.

  • Open images, videos, PDFs, DOCX, spreadsheets, text files, ZIP archives, EPUB eBooks, or paste a URL.
  • OFF / Interact mode lets you scroll, use media controls, interact with embedded content, and move around zoomed documents.
  • Use Pen, Highlighter, Erase, or Laser to annotate, teach, review, or present.
  • Use dropdown launchers for English/Chinese AI tools and English/Chinese social media platforms.
  • The floating panel can be moved, resized, widened, narrowed, made taller/shorter, and scrolled to reveal all tools.
